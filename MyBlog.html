My Daily Routine - 09.01.2022

Hi everyone, happy new year! I hope everyone has a successful 2022 and I though I'd share my daily routine to show how I learn, be inspired, relax, be healthy and generally live a life I want to live. I hope you can take inspiration and let me know your daiily routine, I would love to see if there are any possible improvements I could make!

    • 5.00am: Wake up - I'm definitely a morning person and I feel productive when I first wake up, although this won't be for everyone.
      5.05am: Read
      5.30am: Shower
      5.45am: Breakfast
      6.00am: Morning Walk
      7.45am: Check News, Emails, Relax
      8.30am: Work
      5.00pm: Evening Walk (Longer walks on Fridays due to 4pm finish, which makes a huge difference to my happiness).
      6.00pm: Dinner
      7.00pm: Study (Usually using the pomodoro technique)
      8.30pm: Watch TV, Check News / Social Media, Relax
      9.15pm: Evening Routine (Brush Teeth, Wash Face, etc)
      9.30pm: Go to Bed, Read
      10.00pm: Sleep


    • When working in the office, my routine is slightly different due to the large amount of travelling time and it can be difficult to fit in everything I want to. I see some of the benefits of office working, but after the pandemic forced remote working to become the norm it made me realise how much more time I had to complete all of my activities. I even feel much more rested as a result of not needing to wake up earlier, which as a result is actually making me more productive throughout the day.
